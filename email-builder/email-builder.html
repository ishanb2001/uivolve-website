What is this tool for? ▼

Welcome to the Email Builder! This tool helps you easily create and customize emails. You can add various sections, reorder them, and copy the layout to use in Outlook.

How do I add sections to my email canvas? ▼

To add sections to your email canvas, click on the buttons in the main interface. Each section you add will appear in the side menu, where you can manage and reorder them.

On the right-hand side, you'll see a list of available sections (like text, images, buttons, etc.).

Each option is labeled clearly (e.g., "Add Text," "Add Banner," "Add Footer") and will be added if you click on them.

Each added section will appear in the email preview and will also be listed in the right menu in the order they were added.

Can I reorder the sections I add to the canvas? ▼

In the right menu, you'll see arrows next to each section label (Up and Down arrows).

Click the up or down arrows to move the section up or down in the list.

The changes will be reflected in the email preview on the left, so the sections will be reordered accordingly.

How do I delete a section from my email canvas? ▼

In the right menu, click the "Delete" button next to the section you want to remove.

The section will be deleted from both the email preview and the right menu list.

How do I copy the email to Outlook? ▼

Once you've finished building your email, click the "Copy Formatting" button. Then, go to Outlook, paste the email, and select the "Keep Source Formatting" option to maintain the layout.

Can I customize the sections I add to my email? ▼

Customization of sections is done after pasting the email into Outlook. You can change the text and other elements directly in Outlook to fit your needs.

What image formats are supported for uploads? ▼

You can upload images in JPEG, PNG, and GIF formats.

Why is the email formatted off in Outlook, and how can I fix it? ▼

On a Mac, press Command + V to paste the copied content, then click the Paste Options icon (the clipboard) that appears and select Keep Source Formatting.

On Windows, press Ctrl + V to paste the copied content, then click the Paste Options icon (the clipboard) that appears and choose Keep Source Formatting.

What is this tool for? ▼

The objective of this tool is to help users create customized logo displays for email campaigns. It allows users to easily select from different layout options, drag and drop partner logos into designated placeholders, and arrange them to fit the desired result. Once the logos are positioned, users can save the design as an image, making it perfect for embedding in emails to showcase partnerships, sponsors, or collaborators.

How does this work? ▼

To use the tool, start by clicking on "Logo Spot Maker" located beneath the page header.

Next, click on any of the section buttons in the right menu (such as 'Add One Logo,' 'Add Four Logos Divider,' etc.).

Once you click a button, a section with placeholder images will appear on the canvas to the left.

You can then drag a logo from the "Partner Logos" section into any of the placeholder images.

Finally, save your work by clicking the "Save as Image" button.

How do I add logos to my canvas? ▼

Click on any of the buttons in the right menu depending on how many logos you want to showcase (e.g., 'Add One Logo,' 'Add Four Logos Divider,' etc.). After adding any of these sections to the canvas, drag and drop a logo from the image library (on the right side menu) into any placeholder image within the sections displayed on the canvas.

Can I rearrange the sections on the canvas? ▼

Each menu item in the right menu has arrows beside it, allowing you to reorder the sections dynamically as they are added.

How do I remove a logo from the canvas? ▼

To remove a logo, click the delete button for the respective menu item. This will remove it from your canvas.

How can I adjust the size of a logo? ▼

To adjust the size of a logo, simply select it from the logo library. When you click on the image, you'll see a blue outline appear around it. From there, you can use the "Increase Size" or "Decrease Size" buttons to adjust the logo's dimensions. After resizing, drag the logo back into the placeholder to perfectly fit it within your design. This ensures your logo aligns seamlessly with your layout.

Can I save my logo arrangement as an image? ▼

Yes, once you are satisfied with your logo arrangement, click the "Save as Image" button to export your design. This allows you to use the image in other applications or share it with others.
